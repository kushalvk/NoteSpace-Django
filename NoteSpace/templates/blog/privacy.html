Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At NoteSpace, we are committed to protecting your personal information and giving you transparency and control over your data.

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information you voluntarily provide when you create an account, publish content, or interact with the platform. This includes:

  • Your username, email address, and optional profile details
  • Content you publish (posts, comments, bio)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device info — anonymized where possible)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us deliver and improve NoteSpace:

  • To authenticate and personalize your account
  • To display and distribute your published content
  • To send important service notifications (e.g. password reset)
  • To detect abuse and protect the platform

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and provide core functionality. Optional analytics cookies help us understand how people use NoteSpace so we can make it better.

You can manage or disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

4. Data Protection & Security

We use industry-standard encryption, secure hosting, and regular security audits to protect your data. Your content is stored securely and only accessible to you and authorized platform administrators.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Edit or delete your profile and posts at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Delete your account permanently

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@notespace.in.
